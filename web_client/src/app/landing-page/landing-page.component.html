VERSION CONTROLLED, CLOUD BASED VIDEO STORAGE

Reliable Backup

If you’ve ever had a hard drive corrupt then you know the pain that us and so many other editors have felt. They just aren’t as reliable as we’d like them to be. That’s why Ouvie uses AWS S3 cloud storage to keep your projects safe, which is designed to provide 99.999999999% durability. That’s right, eleven nine’s.

Version Control

Too often we get so far into a project and realise the last few changes just don’t fit, and sometimes the “undo” button just isn’t enough. Ouvie provides its users with robust version control, keeping track of every version of your project right from the very beginning, so you can focus on letting your creative brilliance shine.

Reduced Bandwidth

If you want reliable backup, you save your work in the cloud. It can be such a drag though to repeatedly upload the same file over and over, especially when you’ve only added that one small clip in the middle. With Ouvie, we upload only what you change from version to version, saving you a ton on bandwidth and time.
